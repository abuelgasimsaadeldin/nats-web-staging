We Provide Oil & Gas Products

Welcome to North American Trade Source (NATS), a leading force in the oil and gas industry based in the United States. With a rich legacy of excellence and a commitment to superior service, we specialize in the sourcing, trading, and distribution of high-quality petroleum products and natural gas across North America. Our extensive network and deep industry expertise enable us to deliver reliable and efficient solutions to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

We Provide Consultation

Deciding on the right products for your needs can be a complex process, but at North American Trade Source (NATS), we are here to simplify it for you. Our team of dedicated professionals is highly experienced in providing expert consultation and technical clarification for any of the engineering products or natural gas you may require.

We Take Care of Logistics

Our team will aid you through your purchases and orders when you make a payment. We will be on top of the packing, delivering items to freight forwarders, and making sure they meet all requirements by customs. We work directly with a freight forwarding company to handle the paperwork and arrange transport for your goods.

We Perform Inspection

Knowing the products you order are within technical specifications is important, which is why having the ability to conduct certain types of inspections before delivery is vital. Our team is trained to conduct hydro-testing and material inspections. Quality-checking is also something we do, free of charge, to ensure our products are workable and up to standard.
